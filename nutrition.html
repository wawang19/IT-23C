Nutrition for a Healthy Mind and Body

A balanced diet plays a key role in maintaining mental and physical health.

  • Eat a Variety of Foods: Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your diet.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Avoid Excessive Processed Foods: Limit sugary, fried, and heavily processed foods.
  • Incorporate Omega-3 Fatty Acids: Found in fish, walnuts, and flaxseeds, omega-3s promote brain health.
